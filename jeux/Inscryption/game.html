Inscryption is an interesting indie card game. The immersion arouses curiosity, which is itself rewarded.
The gameplay will always try to guide you in a way to have the best gameplay experience ( maybe sometimes too much ? )
The replayability adds a lot because you directly interact with it every time.
The more you play, the more intrusive the psychological pressure becomes until it breaks the fourth wall.

Damn, I want to create some 3D assets like this

However, despite a very nice excellent first half, I felt that some decision-making was losing impact over the duration...
Especially on the second phase of the game, you get quickly flooded of new cards, new effects without mentioning that you basically switch half percent of the gameplay itself
